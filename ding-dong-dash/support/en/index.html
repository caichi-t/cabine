Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I want to reset my game progress.

When the app is deleted, game progress stored on the device is generally deleted. Behavior may vary depending on the device and OS settings.

Q. I cannot hear any sound.

Please check your device volume, silent mode, Bluetooth connection, and in-app volume settings.

Q. Does the app show ads?

The app uses Unity Ads for advertising. Please see the Privacy Policy for details.

Q. Can I change the language?

You can change to a supported language from the in-app settings screen.

Q. I want to report a bug.

Please contact support@cabine.io with the app name, device, OS version, screen where the issue occurred, and steps to reproduce it.
