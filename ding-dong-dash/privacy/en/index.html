Privacy Policy

This policy explains how information is handled in the game app "Ding Dong Dash".

Effective date: July 10, 2026 / Last updated: July 10, 2026

日本語 English

1. Scope

This policy applies to the mobile game app "Ding Dong Dash" (the "App") and the web page on which this policy is published.

2. Information We Directly Collect

The App does not ask users to provide directly identifying information such as name, email address, phone number, or postal address.

The App stores gameplay progress, cleared stages, selected language, tutorial completion status, and internal ad display counts on the user's device. These items are used to provide App functionality and are not sent to our server.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The App uses Unity Ads for advertising. Unity Ads may collect information such as device information, IP address, advertising identifiers, app usage data, and ad impression, click, and completion data for ad delivery, ad quality, fraud prevention, measurement, legal compliance, and advertising based on user choices.

For details about Unity's handling of information, please review Unity's Game Player and App User Privacy Policy.

Unity Game Player and App User Privacy Policy

4. Purposes of Use

We and third-party services use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing App functionality, saving progress, and retaining the display language
  • Displaying ads, managing ad inventory, and maintaining ad quality
  • Preventing fraud and unauthorized ad activity
  • Responding to bugs, improving services, and handling inquiries
  • Complying with laws, store policies, and advertising network requirements

5. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell or provide directly identifying user information to third parties, except as required by law. However, third-party services integrated into the App may process information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. User Choices

Users may be able to reset advertising identifiers, limit personalized ads, or change app permission settings from their device settings. The names and availability of these settings may vary depending on OS, device, region, and store specifications.

When the App is deleted, local data stored by the App on the device is generally deleted.

7. Children and Minors

The App does not directly collect age, name, contact information, or similar information from users. If a minor uses the App, the minor should do so with the consent and supervision of a parent or guardian. We will respond within a reasonable scope to inquiries from parents or guardians regarding information handling.

8. Security

We strive to take reasonable security measures to help prevent loss, leakage, alteration, unauthorized access, and similar risks regarding information handled by the App.

9. This Web Page

This web page is hosted by GitHub Pages. We do not place our own analytics tools, advertising tags, or cookies on this page. For details about GitHub's handling of information, please review GitHub's privacy statement.

GitHub General Privacy Statement

10. Changes

We may update this policy as needed. If there are material changes, we will announce them on this page. The updated policy becomes effective when posted on this page.
